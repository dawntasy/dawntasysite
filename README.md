# Dawntasy

> Dawntasy is the creative and research umbrella of Jasper Jiang, a 13-year-old published author, AI architect and machine learning engineer. The site covers his debut YA fantasy novel, deep learning work, AI competition results, and newsletter.

Jasper Jiang published his debut novel *Dawntasy: Time's True Name* in January 2025 at age 13, with 100% of proceeds donated to the Make-A-Wish Foundation. He is working on numerous machine learning projects and is authoring a research paper on FunCAN (Functional Complexity Adaptive Networks).
